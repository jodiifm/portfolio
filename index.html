ABOUT ME



Hello, I'm Jodi. I am a graphic design student and aspiring illustrator from Ontario, Canada, but I am currently based in Palawan, Philippines. I am a student at Centennial College working towards getting my advanced diploma in graphic design.

Apart from my passion for design and illustration, you can catch me playing ultimate frisbee in the afternoons, baking something up in the kitchen, or playing around with some chords on the guitar.

My passion for creating art has only grown over the years, along with my desire to tell stories through my illustrations. As a designer, it is important to realize that there is endless growth on this journey of artistic discovery.
